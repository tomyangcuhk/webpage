Tongou Yang (Thomas)
I am an assistant professor at the Department of Mathematics, Southern University of Science and Technology, starting from September 2025.

Previously, I held postdoctoral positions at the University of California, Los Angeles and the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

I obtained a Ph.D. from the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada, where I was supervised by Professor Malabika Pramanik.

I am interested in harmonic analysis and geometric measure theory.
